Add Miller Notes to your phone

Get it on your home screen so it opens full-screen, like a real app.

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the top right.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right corner.
  2. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app).
  3. Tap Add / Install to confirm.
